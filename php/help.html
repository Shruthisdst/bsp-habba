  3. SEARCH: In search page, only single word can be given as an input. It will search that word both in Kannada and English books.
    • The softkeyboard is provided for typing Kannada words. Another softkeyboard is provided for typing diacritical marks.

      Example: The following words that contains diacritical marks.
      ŚrīKṛshṇa, Dīpāvaḷi, Ekādaśī, uttarāshāḍhā and Dhanishṭhā etc...

    • After entering a single word in textbox, click on the "Search" button.
    • After clicking on the "Search" button, it shows the results as a paragraph that contains search word. Click on "More" link to reach full paragraph in a book.
